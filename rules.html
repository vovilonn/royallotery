    Lottery - is a drawing of cash prizes with tickets, based on a self-executive independent algorithm of the random choices of winners (please see the Rules section "Winnings Probability Distribution" ).

    Cash prizes are in the BNB currency.


    Smart contract – independent protocol governing the distribution and charging of lottery winnings. (Smart contract address is specified in the Rules section «Reliability Guarantee» ).

    Randomizer – the algorithm of the random choices of winners, fixed in a smart-contract.

  • Lottery participation requirements

    1)Having a wallet.


    2) Buying a lottery ticket.

  • The ticket purchase

    Click the "Start" button on the main page of the site or by clicking the "Buy more tickets" button from your personal area. Then pay for the ticket.


    You may do it by clicking the "Start" button on the main page of the website or by clicking the "Buy more tickets" button from your personal area.


    The purchased ticket takes part in the next draw.

  • Prize pool

    The prize pool is derived from the tickets sold by the following principle:


    50% of the revenue from one ticket is added to the prize pool of the next draw.


    20% is delivered to the Jackpot prize pool.


    20% go to support the team and the project.


    10% provide a lottery referral program.

  • Drawing

    The draw starts at the scheduled time. After the draw, the date and time of the next draw appear.


    All users who have bought a ticket participate in the draw.


    When the draw begins, the randomizer selects the winners in stages (please see the Rules section «Winnings Probability Distribution» ).

    The disbursement for winners is made immediately at the time of winning.


    The tickets lost in the previous draw allow to apply improvement of the probability of winning to the tickets that participate in the next draw (please see the Rules section «Improving chance of winning» ).

  • Winnings Probability Distribution

    As part of the drawing, the randomizer selects the winners in stages, taking into account the individual probability of winning.


    The first ticket wins the prize of 10% of the cash pool.


    The next 10 tickets fall into the second category. Each ticket wins the prize of 2% of the cash pool


    10% of tickets that have not fallen into the first two categories receive a prize of doubled ticket price (each ticket).


    5% of tickets that have not fallen into the first three categories receive a prize of full ticket price (each ticket).


    10% of tickets that have not fallen into the first four categories receive a prize of half ticket price (each ticket).

  • Improving chance of winning.

    All the tickets lost in the draw allow to increase the probability of winning of the tickets that participate in the next draw.


    One losing ticket allows you to apply +5% of the probability of winning to a new ticket, but not more than 50% for a ticket.


    To improve your chances of winning, go to your personal area to the lottery tickets table and click "apply improvement" near the desired ticket. Next, use the slider to select the available value of increasing the probability.


    To improve all new tickets, click on the "Improve All" button, and the available percentages will be evenly distributed among the new tickets.

  • Jackpot

    The jackpot is accumulated on a separate secure wallet. Its balance is displayed on the main page of the website.


    Every 130 games the administration decides to hold the jackpot draw.


    The date and time of the event are displayed in the announcement after the draw is approved.


    To participate in the Jackpot draw you must play 100 times in the daily (or weekly) lottery.

  • Personal area

    All tickets that have participated or will participate in draws are presented in your personal area.


    In the "My tickets" section, you can manage the probability of winning tickets (please see the Rules section «Improving chance of winning» ) and purchase new tickets.

    The "Referral link" section displays your referral link, the number of referrals and the amount of referral revenue, which can be withdrawn by clicking on the "Withdraw funds" button.

  • Referral program

    The user who follows your referral link and binds his wallet becomes your referral, and you become his referral.


    The referrer will receive a commission by a three-level system (according to the "Referral Incomes" table) each time the referred user purchases a ticket or wins a prize.
